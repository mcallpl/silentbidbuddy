- **Organization Type**: 501(c)(3) Non-profit charity

## 5. TYPOGRAPHY RECOMMENDATIONS

### 5.1 Font Families

**Heading Font (Professional, Clean)**
- Primary: **Segoe UI**, Tahoma, sans-serif
- Alternative: **Roboto**, **Open Sans**
- Usage: Page titles, section headings, navigation
- Weight: 600-700 (Semi-bold to Bold)
- Character: Clear, modern, accessible, trustworthy

**Body Font (Readable, Accessible)**
- Primary: **Segoe UI**, Tahoma, sans-serif
- Alternative: **Roboto**, **Lato**, **Source Sans Pro**
- Usage: Body text, descriptions, content
- Weight: 400 (Regular)
- Size: 14-16px base
- Line-height: 1.5-1.6 for readability

**Accent Font (Nonprofit Branding)**
- Primary: **Segoe UI Bold**, sans-serif
- Usage: Call-to-action buttons, emphasis text
- Weight: 700 (Bold)

## 12. ACCESSIBILITY STANDARDS (WCAG 2.1 AA)

### 12.1 Color Contrast Requirements
- **Body text on white**: Minimum 4.5:1 contrast (use #212121)
- **Large text (18px+) on white**: Minimum 3:1 contrast
- **Green accent (#2E7D32) on white**: 8:1 contrast (meets AA+)
- **All interactive elements**: Minimum 3:1 contrast for distinguishability

### 12.2 Color Independence
- **Do not rely solely on color** to convey information
- **Use icons** with text labels alongside color-coded elements
- **Patterns/textures**: Consider adding subtle patterns to distinguish colored sections
- **Status indicators**: Combine color with icons (✓ for success, ! for alert, etc.)

### 12.3 Font Recommendations
- **Minimum size**: 12px for body text (16px preferred)
- **Font smoothing**: Use -webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased
- **Letter spacing**: Increase to 0.12em for large text
- **Line height**: Maintain 1.5+ for readability

## 14. PHOTO & IMAGE GUIDELINES

### 14.1 Photography Style
- **Aesthetic**: Warm, human-centered, genuine emotion
- **Subjects**: People in community settings, celebration moments, support groups
- **Tone**: Hopeful, inclusive, professional
- **Lighting**: Natural daylight preferred, warm indoor lighting
- **Composition**: Rule of thirds, intentional framing

### 14.2 Image Requirements
- **Format**: JPG (photographs), PNG (graphics), SVG (icons)
- **Quality**: Minimum 72 dpi for web, preferably 300 dpi source
- **Dimensions**: Optimize for web (compress without visible quality loss)
- **Alt Text**: Descriptive, meaningful alt attributes for accessibility
- **Aspect Ratios**:
  - Hero images: 16:9 (1920x1080px)
  - Card images: 1:1 (400x400px)
  - Portrait photos: 3:4 (300x400px)

## 17. BRAND CONSISTENCY GUIDELINES

### 17.1 Do's
✓ Use official green (#2E7D32) for primary brand elements  
✓ Maintain consistent spacing and padding throughout  
✓ Use professional typography hierarchy  
✓ Combine color with icons/patterns for clarity  
✓ Reserve gold (#F57C00) for event-specific celebration elements  
✓ Use white space effectively for elegance  
✓ Keep messaging clear, compassionate, and mission-focused  

### 17.2 Don'ts
✗ Do NOT use random green shades - stick to #2E7D32 and approved variations  
✗ Do NOT use shadows without purpose - maintain minimalist aesthetic  
✗ Do NOT mix serif and sans-serif fonts unpurposefully  
✗ Do NOT create ultra-small text - maintain accessibility  
✗ Do NOT overuse gold - reserve for celebration/milestone elements  
✗ Do NOT use gradients excessively - keep professional  
✗ Do NOT ignore contrast requirements
